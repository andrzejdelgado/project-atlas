## Show, don't tell

These components were designed for thumbs, not mice — so rather than show static screenshots, each one lives inside a phone you can operate directly. On desktop, your cursor becomes a touch point the moment it enters the screen: press, drag and swipe exactly as you would in the device mode of Chrome's inspector. On a real phone, just use your finger.
